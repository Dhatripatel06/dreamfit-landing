The workflow

Up and running in 4 steps

BoutiqueWare replaces your notebook, WhatsApp group, and paper job cards — with one platform your whole team can use.

01

Create order & add garments

Add customer details, measurements, reference photos (studio images, customer images, reference cloth), price range, and delivery date. One screen, done in 2 minutes.

02

Set production workflow

Choose from Standard, Purchase, or Aari templates — or build a custom sequence. Add stages like Embroidery, Ironing, Packing to any order.

03

Workers scan QR to advance stages

Print the job card with QR code. Tailor scans it → marks Cutting done → your dashboard updates live. No app download needed for workers.

04

Invoice auto-sent on delivery

Mark order delivered → balance calculated → invoice sent to customer's WhatsApp with job card + reference images. No follow-up calls.

📱
QR-Powered Production
